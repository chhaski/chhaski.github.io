study in choreography for the grid:

a duet

"A Study in Choreography for the Grid: A Duet" spans three digital sites: a 9-channel virtual-gallery video installation, an interactive website, and a temporary instagram account. The piece is inspired by Maya Deren's "A Study in Choreography for Camera" (1945) among others. It is a formal study of movement both framed and broken by the grid and of the erotics produced by this framing.
