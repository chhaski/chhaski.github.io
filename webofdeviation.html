The force of desire is productive in its ability to reorient, fuel and generate alternate landscapes. But desire is not a one-way street. We both make places and are made by them through a continuous co-constitutive, relational process.

How does desire create surfaces and boundaries? How are we shaped by its e/affects? How does governed space prescript our movement? How do the structures in our lives make some things reachable and others out of reach? How might we craft insurgent desire paths toward more just and pleasurable worlds?
