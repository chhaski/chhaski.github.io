binding study explores possible intimacies mediated by technological devices

collaboration with Pedro Sodre, special thanks to Nuntinee Tansrisakul


Recorded at Prospect Park’s Vale of Cashmere, the performers engage in various walking choreographies, tethered to a multi-part audio set-up that allows them to hear each other's breath and sounds. In one hand, each of the performers holds a shotgun directional mic, which when pointed in the direction of the other performer, captures the sound of the other’s body and movement.
