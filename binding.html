binding study [interospection]

binding study is a video documenting a live performance using biofeedback [in collaboration with Pedro Sodre with special thanks to Nuntinee Tansrisakul]

Recorded at Prospect Park’s Vale of Cashmere, the performers engage in various walking choreographies, each tethered to a multi-part audio set-up that allows them to hear the other's breath and sounds. In one hand, each of the performers holds a shotgun directional mic, which when pointed in the direction of the other performer, captures the sound of the other’s body and movement.
