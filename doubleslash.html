Project Title: // "double slash"
By immersing audiences in the 'then and there' of the archive, the project invites reflection on how such engagements can rupture the impasse of the 'here and now'. "double slash" grapples with the archive's role in disrupting our sense of material, spatial, and temporal continuity. Maneuvering discontinuity, the piece asks questions about loss and belonging. The project explores the fragility of digitality, drawing on theories of memory, software, and the ephemeral nature of digital media.
