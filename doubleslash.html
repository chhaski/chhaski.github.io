archive practice (media archaeology)

Engaging the 'then and there' of a neglected family super 8 video archive, the documentation below tracks my exploration of the material through various migrations of format and logical context. Following the material through these conceptual rewirings, they are interested in the political and poetic dynamics of the archive reanimated through manoeuvrings of the material's discontinuity and disintegration.
