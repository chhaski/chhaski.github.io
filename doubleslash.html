archive practice (media archaeology)

Working with a neglected family super 8 video archive (shot by my aunt in the 1980s in Peru), the documentation below tracks my exploration of the material through several migrations of format and logical context. Following the material through these conceptual rewirings, they are interested in the political and poetic dynamics of the archive reanimated through manoeuvrings of the material's discontinuity and disintegration.
